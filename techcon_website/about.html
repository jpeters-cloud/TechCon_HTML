Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup for developers and technologists. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most influential technology conferences in the world, hosting thousands of attendees annually across various cities.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, empower collaboration, and accelerate the future of technology through education and community. We believe in open access to knowledge, diversity in tech, and the power of human creativity.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the brightest minds in the tech industry:

Dr. Ada Lin

Renowned AI researcher and author, Dr. Lin shared groundbreaking insights into ethical AI and its future implications in society.

Marcus O'Neil

As CTO of a major tech startup, Marcus discussed emerging trends in cloud computing and cybersecurity in his keynote.
